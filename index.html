Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our password generator.

Is this password generator really free?

Yes — it is 100% free to use, with no signup, no ads, and no limits on how many passwords you generate.

Are my generated passwords stored anywhere?

No. All password generation happens locally in your browser using the Web Crypto API. Nothing is ever sent to our servers.

What makes a password strong?

A strong password is long (16+ characters), uses a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols, and is unique to each account. Our tool makes generating one effortless.

Can I use the same password everywhere?

No — please don't. Reusing passwords means one data breach can compromise all your accounts. Use a unique password per service, and consider a password manager.

Does this work offline?

Once the page is loaded, yes. All generation logic lives in the browser, so it continues to work even without an internet connection.
