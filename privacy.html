Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information — if any — is collected when you use Password Generator (the "Service"), and how we handle it.

Passwords You Generate

All password generation happens entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API. We never see, transmit, log, or store any password you generate. They exist only on your device, in your browser's memory.

Information We Collect

We do not require accounts, logins, or any personal information to use this Service.

Our web host and any third-party analytics (if present) may collect standard technical data such as browser type, operating system, approximate region, and page views — aggregated and anonymous — to help us improve the site. No personally identifying information is collected.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. Any cookies set are strictly essential for the website to function.

Third-Party Services

We load the Inter font from Google Fonts to keep the site lightweight. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle requests.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
