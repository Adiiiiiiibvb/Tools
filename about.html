About Password Generator

A simple tool with a single goal — to make creating strong, unique passwords effortless for everyone.

Our Mission

Weak and reused passwords are the single biggest cause of account compromises online. We built Password Generator to solve that — with a tool that is free, fast, private, and works on every device.

What We Believe

  • Security should be simple. Strong passwords should be one click away, not a chore.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable. Your passwords should never leave your device.
  • Good tools should be free. No signups, no paywalls, no tricks.

How We Build It

Our generator uses the Web Crypto API built directly into modern browsers — the same cryptographic standard used by banks and security-critical applications. Because it runs 100% in your browser, we never see, transmit, or store a single character of your passwords.

Who It's For

From individuals setting up a new email account to professionals managing dozens of logins, anyone who wants a quick, reliable way to create strong passwords will feel at home here.
